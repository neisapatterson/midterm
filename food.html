Grand Del Mar

Food freshley prepared for you!




Here are Grand Del Mar, we treat our customers with the upmost quality food we can provide. Fresh produce, freshly kneaded pasta dough, locally grown fruites and veggies, you name it, we got it. With all room packages, a complimentary breakfast is provided free of charge. Enjoy our hot bar, hot breakfast foods (including but not limited to pancakes, eggs made your way , bacon, oatmeal, etc.), freshed pressed fruit juices and even more.




Our speciality lunch services is available to everyone for purchase but included in the family package. During the lunch services, our menu is never the same. Our chef's are inspired to get creative with their dishes. Food can be order freshly from the offered menu or if you have a special request, the chef's are more than happy to cook per request. An open salad and soup bar is included during the lunch period.




Our dinner is award winning across all resorts in the Miami area. Not only does the dinner include everything that the lunch service provides, but you also get access to our unlimited sushi bar, mochi icecream bar, hotpot station, and Korean BBQ pits. Dinner also comes with access to our open bar open 24 hours.
